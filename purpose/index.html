There are a lot of people out there. What are they all doing? We're chatting them up to find out.

What are you doing today? How did you get here? What do you look forward to? Who do you live with? What's your favorite part of your day?

On a Wednesday is an attempt to collect a few of those stories. A few questions and a photo: a sneak peek into someone's life.

We ask questions on Wednesdays and publish interviews on Wednesdays.

Have any questions or comments? Email us at info@onawednesday.com
